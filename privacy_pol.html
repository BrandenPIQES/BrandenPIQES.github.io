Privacy policy for Connect_tutoring

Last updated: 27 Jan 2024

Connect_tutoring operates https://connect-tutoring.pages.dev/ (the "Site"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information we receive from students who are part of the program.

Information Collection and Use

We may collect the following types of information from our users:

1. Personal Information

When students enroll in our tutoring programs, we may collect personal information such as their name, age, contact information, and academic background.

2. Academic Progress Information

At the end of each academic term, we collect information regarding students' academic progress, including marks and assessments. This information is used for the purpose of evaluating the effectiveness of our tutoring programs and tailoring future sessions to meet individual student needs.

Use of Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

Personal Information

  • To provide tutoring services tailored to each student's needs.
  • To communicate with students, parents, and guardians regarding scheduling, progress, and other relevant information.
  • To improve our tutoring programs and services.

Academic Progress Information

  • To assess the effectiveness of our tutoring programs.
  • To compare students' academic progress over different terms and identify areas for improvement.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to ensure the security of your personal information and academic progress data. This includes encryption, secure storage, and access controls to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.
