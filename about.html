About us

Welcome to Connect Tutoring, a passionate initiative under the umbrella of the RNDA. Our mission is rooted in the belief that education is the key to unlocking boundless potential, and we are committed to making a lasting impact on the lives of students.

Our Vision

At Connect Tutoring, we envision a world where every student has the opportunity to thrive academically, regardless of their background or circumstances. We believe that education is a powerful tool for social change, and by providing quality tutoring, we aim to empower students to reach their full potential and build a brighter future.

Our Mission

Our primary mission is to provide high-quality tutoring services to students at Sinethemba High School and beyond. Currently focusing on Accounting, Physical Sciences, and Mathematics, we aim to bridge the educational gap, cultivate a love for learning, and inspire students to pursue their academic and career goals with confidence.

What Sets Us Apart

Dedicated Tutors

Our team of dedicated tutors is passionate about education and committed to nurturing a supportive learning environment. They bring a wealth of knowledge, expertise, and a personalized approach to cater to the unique needs of each student.

Holistic Approach

Connect Tutoring believes in a holistic approach to education. We not only focus on academic excellence but also strive to instill essential life skills, foster critical thinking, and promote a love for continuous learning.

Community Engagement

We understand the importance of community engagement in fostering positive change. Beyond tutoring, we actively engage with the community, collaborate with schools, and seek partnerships to create a network that supports students' educational journeys.

Expanding Horizons

While our roots are in Sinethemba High School, we aspire to expand our reach and impact by extending our tutoring services to other schools. By doing so, we hope to create a ripple effect that transforms not only individual lives but entire communities.
